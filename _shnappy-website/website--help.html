Frequently Asked Questions about Pricing

How does the free trial work?

We offer a fully featured 30 day free trial for you to get started. Once you are ready, you can then pick a plan and start your paid subscription. If your trial expires, your account becomes inactive. You can reactivate your account and start your subscription later as well.

Can I change plans later?

Yep! You can upgrade/downgrade your plan any time to suit your needs. Your payments are adjusted on a pro-rated basis.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Paypal and New Zealand bank transfer.

Do I need a Dropbox account?

You do indeed - Shnappy keeps your photos safe in your Dropbox. The free Dropbox plan is perfect.

Do I need a website/hosting service?

Nope - your Shnappy portfolio is hosted on our fully managed, fast and secure cloud platform that leverages the world's best infrastructure services.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel at any point - though we'll be sad to see you go! Once you cancel, your client galleries will no longer be accessible.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Of course! If you're not completely happy with Shnappy in your first 30 days we'll refund any payments made by you.

How long are your contracts?

We don't do contracts! You're free to stay as long as you'd like.
